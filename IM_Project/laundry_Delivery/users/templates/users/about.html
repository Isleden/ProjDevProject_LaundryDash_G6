Our History

Founded in 2024, LaundryDash began with a vision to provide top-quality laundry and dry cleaning services. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted name, offering convenience and reliability to thousands of happy customers.

At LaundryDash, we’re about more than just laundry — we’re about making lives easier.

Our Mission

  • Supporting the community by providing jobs and contributing to a greener future.
  • Building lasting relationships with our customers.
